============================
Product Overview
============================

Mattermost allows organizations to achieve their highest goals through enterprise-grade communication, 
and to enjoy all the productivity benefits of workplace messaging across web, 
mobile, 
and PC.
This is coupled with unlimited archiving, 
searching,
and integrating in a single-tenant, 
private cloud solution under IT control.

Mattermost is used in 14 languages by thousands of organizations around the world and is valued for its unmatched benefits in security,
configurability,
and scalability.

Security
~~~~~~~~-

Mattermost keeps vital communications like access to mobile and desktop apps behind your firewall and can be deployed using `dozens of security features <https://docs.mattermost.com/overview/security.html>`_ vetted by top security researchers. 
Data stays on servers you control and is encrypted using keys you control.

Configurability 
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~-

Mattermost allows organizations to adapt deployments to their needs, 
preferences, 
and existing systems. 
The open source Mattermost server enables advanced white labelling and customization with complete access to RESTful server APIs, 
drivers, 
webhooks, 
and hundreds of third party extensions.

Scalability 
~~~~~~~~~~~~

Mattermost can scale from dozens to tens of thousands of users on the same server. 
The Mattermost E20 offers cluster-based high availability deployments with horizontal scaling and advanced performance monitoring atop single-tenant infrastructure with high performance.


About the Mattermost Open Source Project
----------------------------------------------

Mattermost is an open source, 
private cloud alternative to proprietary SaaS messaging for teams. 
The software has been developed in partnership with over 500 contributors from around the world and easily rivals the productivity of SaaS solutions. This productivity is achieved with superior control, 
data sovereignty, 
configurability, 
freedom from lock-in, 
and enhanced security that keeps vital communication behind your firewall.

Roadmap
~~~~~~~

The Mattermost roadmap is influenced by community contributors and drawn by staff at Mattermost, Inc.
Organizations with needs beyond the scope of the open source project can tap into the commercial *Enterprise Edition* extensions available from Mattermost, Inc. 
The partnership with a core committer community coupled with revenue from the *Enterprise Edition* ensures the continued improvement of all editions.

Mattermost Editions
-----------------------

Mattermost is offered in three editions:

Mattermost Team Edition
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

The Team Edition is an open source *virtual office* for your team where you get things done day-to-day under MIT license. It includes open source native mobile and desktop apps, 
continuous archiving, 
unlimited search, 
threaded messaging, 
and custom emojis.
It has full access to webhooks, 
APIs, 
drivers,
and third party integrations. 

.. note::
To get started, `download the open source Mattermost Team Edition server <https://about.mattermost.com/download>`_ under an MIT license.

Team Edition Highlights
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Team Edition deploys as a single Linux binary with MySQL or PostgreSQL under an MIT license. Features include:

- One-to-one and group messaging, file sharing and unlimited search history
- Native apps for iOS, Android, Windows, Mac, Linux
- Threaded messaging, emojis and custom emojis
- Highly customizable `third party bots, integrations <https://about.mattermost.com/community-applications/#publicApps>`_ and `command line tools <https://docs.mattermost.com/administration/command-line-tools.html>`_
- Extensive integration support through `webhooks, APIs, drivers <https://docs.mattermost.com/guides/integration.html>`_ and `third party extensions <https://about.mattermost.com/default-app-directory/>`_
- Easily scalable from dozens to hundreds of users
- New improvements released every two months
- Languages include U.S. English, Chinese (Simplified and Traditional), Dutch, French, German, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Polish, Brazilian Portuguese, Russian, Turkish, and Spanish.

advanced access controls,

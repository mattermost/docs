Set language
------------

In a board, set your preferred language by selecting the gear icon |gear| next to your profile picture, then go to **Set language** to apply your language settings.  Language settings in Boards are independent from language settings in Channels.

Random emoji icons
------------------

To enable or disable random emoji icons for your board and cards, select the gear icon |gear| next to your profile picture, then toggle **Random icons on or off**.

Product tour
------------

If you skipped the product tour or want a refresher on Boards, you can restart the product tour by going to the gear icon |gear| next to your profile picture and selecting **Product tour**. This will add a new **Welcome to Boards** template to your sidebar and kick-off the guided onboarding tour.

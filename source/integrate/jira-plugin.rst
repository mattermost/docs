Integrate Jira into Mattermost
===============================

.. include:: ../_static/badges/ent-cloud-selfhosted.rst
  :start-after: :nosearch:


Minimize distractions, reduce context switching between your project management tool and your communication platform integrating Jira with Mattermost. Create Jira issues directly from Mattermost conversations, discuss issues, brainstorm solutions, and follow up on action items in real-time, without leaving Mattermost.
